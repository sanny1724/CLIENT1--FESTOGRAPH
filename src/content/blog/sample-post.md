Hyderabad offers a blend of historic charm and modern elegance, making it a perfect backdrop for pre-wedding shoots. Here are our top picks:

1.  **Golconda Fort**: For a regal, historic vibe.
2.  **Taramati Baradari**: Stunning architecture with great acoustic history.
3.  **Botanical Gardens**: Lush greenery for a natural look.
4.  **Ramoji Film City**: Endless themes and sets.
5.  **Chowmahalla Palace**: Nizami grandeur at its finest.
